NFT .

What Exactly is it?

NFT is like a new Instagram trend, but way deeper, and more impactful. It is a technology believed to revolutionize the society, while accelerating climatic changes at the same instant. NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token. NFTs turn our digital assets into one of a kind, by creating a unique digital signature which defines our ownership of our assets, and that can be bought and sold for real money, crypto currency or NFTs.

Everything that is interchangeable is fungible. Non-fungible tokens mean that they are not interchangeable. It is a digital coin with unique attributes attached to it. Each of them represents unique assets owned by a specific person. Fungible tokens, on the contrary are interchangeable, and can be divided into smaller units to form the same value. Stating an example, a 2000-rupee note is fungible as it can be exchanged for ten 200-rupee notes, or even two hundred 10-rupee notes. But the painting I painted last weekend is non-fungible, as it can not be generated in bulk. Even if it is copied, it won’t stay authentic.

Is it safe?

Each NFT contains distinguishable information about who owns the asset, i.e. the owner, and who sold that asset to the owner, i.e. the seller. This makes the NFT distinct and easily verifiable. Since it is impossible to forge such a certificate, my painting stays original, as NFT secured its originality.

After knowing about the ‘whats’, it is psychological to be curious about the ‘hows’. NFT creates a blockchain-based digital certificate for our digital collectibles, including games, art, and music. This certificate gives your artwork a unique identity. The underlying technology and programming used by the NFTs are the same as the other cryptocurrencies. NFTs majorly exist on Etherium blockchain, a distributed public ledger that records all transactions. Bitcoin and Etherium are however fungible, which means on trading them from one another, we get basically the same item in return, however if we trade NFT we might end up getting something surprisingly different in return. CryptoPunks is one such instance of an NFT.

NFTs have immensely proven to increase sales, as well as have been proven to be a boon in the live of many. Vignesh Sunderesan a.k.a. ‘Metakovan’ recently bought 69.3 million dollars-worth of art on Beeple. Owing to its increasing popularity, people are willing to pay hundreds of dollars for NFTs.

NFTs have enhanced media exposure. This popularity is creating numerous new opportunities, especially for small businesses or entrepreneurs, while promoting copyright and originality of digital assets.

experts in the crypto-industry say that 40 per cent of new crypto-users will use NFT as their entry-point. As a result of its growing popularity, NFT could represent a more significant part of the digital economy in near future.
